About

OMEGA is a Swiss watchmaker based in Biel, Switzerland. Founded in 1848 by Louis Brandt, Omega is now part of the Swatch Group. Now OMEGA is recognized worldwide brand, it has a number of certifications, which brings presence and security for the customer. Known for its tradition and the exclusive world, it offers many services and privileges to its customers.

OMEGA is embarking on a new adventure, a range of 5 products will be launched in April 2015 , joining luxury, modernity and technology is a revolution !
